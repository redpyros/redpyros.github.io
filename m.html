redpyros Profile

redsypros platform is a research and development based company. We deliver customized software solutions which are developed by integrating state of the art technology and achievements in data science, engineering, augmented and virtual reality including, but not limited to solutions for health, medicine, security, energy, education and finance. Also, we develop mobile applications and geospatial solutions.

We are experienced in consultancy, research driven development, event organization and publishing our achievements for sharing innovative outcomes whenever necessary. Our group has data science and engineering researchers and developers from different countries including Canada, USA, Ireland and Turkey. We have over 30 year experience in industry and academia. During the time, we have contributed to a large number of industrial projects which are widely used by our industrial partners. Our discoveries are mostly novel and have been published in over 500 hundred articles and edited many books. We have also organized many activities related to the underlying areas where we gather internationally recognized scholars to discuss and share their latest achievements and innovations. Our clients will enjoy benefiting from this unique vision which distinguishes our team.

Our Expertise and Distinction

Research driven development of fully functional proprietary systems for data collection, analytics and visualization

Our customers will benefit from our team’s distinguished capabilities in developing complete solutions as sophisticated systems for all types of analysis, including descriptive, diagnostic, preventive, predictive and prescriptive. We go beyond the usual trend by building our development on the outcome from extensive research efforts to meet our customers proprietary needs to their full satisfaction beyond their expectations.

We can help our customers stay ahead of their competitors by diversifying and expanding their data sources to cover streams from social media and other external sources leading to dynamic and comprehensive analysis Indeed, the immediate benefit of recent development in intelligent analysis is having data realized more as a valuable resource for non-traditional discoveries to better guide the decision-making process at large.

Regardless of data sources, volume, coverage, completeness, etc. our team is capable of harvesting valuable nuggets which could enrich the vision of decision-makers. Visualization is integrated in the process for faster and better understanding of the data and the results. We are ready to help customers in developing fully functional systems for inhouse data collection and analysis. We work closely with our customers during and beyond the development to ensure their satisfaction from every step throughout the process for them to have a complete system installed and continuously functional with full capacity and comprehensive coverage.

Our team has extensive and comprehensive current experience in using state of art technology and techniques to develop systems that can serve a wide range of applications, including Healthcare solutions: from systems bioinformatics and clinical data management and analysis to virtual and augmented reality for treatment and monitoring of hospitalized patients to remote monitoring of patients with chronic disease

Homeland security: from biometric based applications to early warning systems
Financial data analysis: from exchange rates tracking and management to stock market analysis and portfolio management.
Risk, health and safety: from job site planning and monitoring to optimal evacuation plans in case of emergency in oil and gas, mines, construction sites, etc.
Recommendation and matching: from employment management to customer behavior analysis and satisfaction.
